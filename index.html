Research Focus

Our research group, comprising undergraduates, graduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and faculty, is dedicated to pioneering advancements in materials science. Our primary research focuses on the development of novel semiconductors and carbon-based nanomaterials for applications in energy conversion, energy storage, and sensor technology.

implementing inquiry-based learning to enhance the education of diverse student populations.
